Can fire damage in Oakland be fully restored?
In most cases, yes. Our certified technicians restore fire and smoke damage completely — from structural repairs to odor elimination.
Does insurance cover fire damage restoration in Oakland?
Fire damage is one of the most commonly covered perils in standard homeowner's policies. We confirm your policy is active and work directly with your adjuster.
How long does fire damage restoration take in Oakland?
Scope varies widely. Minor smoke damage may resolve in days. Full structural rebuilds can take weeks. We set a clear timeline after our initial assessment.
What is the difference between fire damage and smoke damage?
Fire damage is physical — burned materials and structural loss. Smoke damage is chemical — soot and odor penetrate surfaces invisibly and continue to damage property if untreated.
Do you handle board-up and emergency securing in Oakland?
Yes — emergency securing is our first step. We protect your property immediately while restoration planning begins.
Can smoke odor be fully eliminated from a Oakland home?
Yes. We use thermal fogging, ozone treatment, and HEPA air scrubbers to fully eliminate smoke odor — not just mask it.
What should I not do after a fire in my Oakland home?
Do not enter until the fire marshal has cleared the property. Do not attempt to clean soot yourself — improper cleaning sets stains permanently. Call us first.
