How It Works

Three steps from damage to restoration — we stay with you through the entire process.

1

You Call Us

Reach out 24/7 by phone or online. Tell us what happened — water, fire, mold, or more. We verify your ownership and active coverage right away.

2

We Manage Your Claim

We become your point of contact with your insurance carrier — coordinating the claim process, documentation, and communication on your behalf.

3

Expert Dispatched

We connect you with a vetted, licensed local contractor and work directly with your insurer on billing — so you can focus on your family, not the paperwork.
