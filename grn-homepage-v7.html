Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does Guardian Restoration Network serve?
We serve Oregon, Washington, and California — 103 cities and growing. We dispatch to all cities with populations over 50,000.
Do you work with insurance companies?
Yes. We confirm your policy is active, document all damage, and work directly with your insurance adjuster. You handle your deductible — we handle the rest.
How fast do you respond to emergencies?
We dispatch immediately upon contact for emergency services including water damage, fire damage, storm damage, and sewage backup.
What does "coverage confirmed fast" mean?
Before work begins, we verify that your homeowner's policy is active and identify what is covered. We do not guarantee specific coverage amounts — that is determined by your insurer — but we confirm your policy is in force and work to document your loss completely.
