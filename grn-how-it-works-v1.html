THE PROCESS

Four Steps from Call to Complete

1
Step One

You Call Guardian

Call (888) 625-0666 any time — day or night, weekday or weekend. A Guardian specialist answers immediately. You describe the damage and provide your insurance information. That's it. We take it from there.

We handle: water damage, fire damage, mold, storm damage, biohazard cleanup, asbestos abatement, and general contractor reconstruction. If insurance covers it, we can help.
2
Step Two

Coverage Confirmed Fast

We confirm your policy is active. We verify you are the property owner and that your coverage applies to your specific type of damage. No guesswork, no runaround. We confirm your coverage is in place before dispatching anyone.

You are responsible for your deductible per your policy. We confirm your policy is active and coverage is confirmed fast — we handle the rest of the process on your behalf.
3
Step Three

We Manage Your Claim

Guardian becomes your single point of contact with your insurance carrier. We handle documentation, coordinate claim communication, and work directly with your insurer on billing. You don't have to deal with your insurance company — we do that for you.

Most homeowners don't know what their insurance policy actually covers, what documentation is required, or how to push back when claims are underpaid. That's our job — not yours.
4
Step Four

Expert Dispatched

A vetted local contractor from our nationwide network is dispatched to your property to begin the restoration work. Every contractor in our network is licensed, insured, and specialized for the type of damage you have.

Contractor dispatch is subject to availability in your area. We always dispatch the nearest qualified contractor to minimize your response time.
