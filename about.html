THE NETWORK MODEL

How the Network Works

Guardian operates as a national coordination layer between homeowners, insurance carriers, and local restoration contractors.

🏠

Homeowner Layer

You call Guardian when damage occurs. We handle intake, confirm your coverage is active, and become your single point of contact throughout the entire claim and restoration process.

🛡️

Insurance Layer

Guardian acts as your liaison with your insurance carrier. We manage documentation, coordinate claim communication, and work directly with your carrier on billing so you don't have to.

🔧

Contractor Layer

We dispatch vetted, licensed local contractors from our nationwide vendor network to perform the physical restoration work. Every contractor is screened before joining our network.

OUR STANDARDS

How We Vet Contractors

Every contractor in the Guardian network meets our standards before being admitted and dispatched to your property.

  • ✓

    Licensed & Insured

    All contractors must hold valid state licenses and carry appropriate insurance coverage for the work they perform.

  • ✓

    Restoration Specialized

    Contractors are vetted for specific expertise in their service area — water, fire, mold, biohazard, asbestos, or general contracting.

  • ✓

    Local to Your Area

    We always dispatch the nearest qualified contractor in our network to minimize response time and travel.

  • ✓

    Performance Monitored

    Contractor performance is tracked across jobs. Contractors who don't meet our standards are removed from the network.
